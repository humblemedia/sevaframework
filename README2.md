## Notes

- All examples have been sanitized to remove personally identifying information
- Timestamps and specific quotes may be redacted
- These are meant to demonstrate output format, not to be used as evidence

## Adding Your Own Examples

If you run a Seva analysis and want to contribute:

1. Ensure the target is a public organization (not an individual)
2. Redact any personal information about non-public figures
3. Include methodology notes
4. Open a PR with your example

## Disclaimer

These examples are pattern analyses, not legal determinations. "Signals, not verdicts."
